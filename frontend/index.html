🌾
Our Story

From One Farm to Thousands of Homes

Milqu Fresh was founded in 2021 by Vikram Nair, a third-generation dairy farmer from Karnataka who grew up watching his family's pure milk get adulterated before reaching city consumers.

Frustrated by the broken supply chain, Vikram went direct — partnering with 50+ trusted local farms and building a cold-chain logistics network that delivers within 6 hours of milking.

Today, Milqu Fresh serves 500+ families across Bangalore, delivering milk, vegetables, fruits and dairy by-products with the same commitment to purity.

🎯 Our Mission

Make farm-fresh produce accessible for every household, while ensuring fair wages for farmers.

🌟 Our Vision

A future where every family knows exactly where their food comes from — and trusts it completely.
